Frequently Asked Questions

How many hours until the weekend?

Our tool provides a real-time countdown to the weekend, showing you the exact days, hours, minutes, and seconds left until you are off the clock on Friday.

Is there a free holiday countdown timer?

Yes, this website includes a free holiday countdown timer that automatically shows you the time remaining until the next major U.S. public holiday.

How do I calculate my workday countdown?

Simply enter your work start and end times in the settings, and our workday countdown clock will automatically calculate and display the time left in your day.

Can I customize my work hours?

Absolutely. Click the settings icon to adjust your work start time and end time to match your personal schedule. The countdown timers will update instantly.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. All your settings, such as salary and work hours, are stored exclusively in your own browser's local storage. No data is ever sent to or stored on a server.

Do I need to create an account to use this tool?

No account is needed. This is a free and open tool that works instantly upon visiting the page. All features are available to everyone without a login.
