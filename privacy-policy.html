Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Workday Countdown Timer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

The Workday Countdown Timer is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All data you provide, including:

  • Annual salary information
  • Work start and end times
  • Any other settings preferences

is stored exclusively in your web browser's local storage on your own device.

3. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not use your information for any purpose. The data stored locally in your browser is used solely to:

  • Display personalized countdown timers
  • Calculate real-time earnings
  • Remember your preferences between sessions

4. Data Storage and Security

All your data remains on your local device. We employ modern web security practices, but ultimately the security of your locally stored data depends on your browser and device security settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect anonymous usage data:

  • Google Fonts for typography
  • Tailwind CSS for styling
  • Lucide for icons

These services may collect standard internet log information and usage details in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

6. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. The Service only uses browser local storage to remember your settings, which is essential for the functionality of the application.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not store your data on our servers, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • Clear your browser's local storage at any time
  • Modify your settings through the application's interface
  • Stop using the Service at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our website.
